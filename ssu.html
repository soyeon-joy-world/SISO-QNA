Frequently Asked Questions

When you receive the U-saint ID (your student number) and password from your buddy later, you can log in.
After that, follow these steps: Management of school affairs > 수강신청/교과과정 > Display personal course schedule

There is one restaurant called 'Student Dining' on the 3rd floor of the "Student Union" building. It's priced at 5000 won, and you can choose from three menu options.
Another restaurant, 'Dodam,' is located on the 2nd floor of "Shinnyang Hall". The price is 6000 won, and it mainly offers Korean dishes.
Please refer to the map for exact locations.

Near school there are some bedding stores or go to grocery stores like E-mart, you can get bedding and towels or something you need also.

You can enter the library using the QR code in Soongsil Univ Library App. Follow these steps:
1. Log in. ID: your student number, PW: Date birth (YYMMDD)
2. Click "Mobile pass"
3. Scan the QR code and enter.

You need to make reservation when you use Soongsil Square ON(2F), Open Study Room(2F) and Maru Study Room(6F). You can't use Graduate Student Study Room(4F).
You can make reservation through Library App or Kiosk in front of there.
App: Click "Study room" -> Click where you want to use -> Choose the seat you want among the available ones.
Kiosk: Click where you want to use -> Choose the seat you want among the available ones.

If you have more questions, feel free to ask your buddy.
